# Protocol v1.2 Progression Summary

Date: 2026-04-08

## Significance of the Move from v1.1 to v1.2

The significance of `v1.2` is not that it replaced `v1.1`.

The significance is that `v1.1` and `v1.2` now form a clear two-stage argument:

1. `v1.1` proves the foundation is real.
2. `v1.2` proves where that foundation was still imprecise.

That is a much stronger research and engineering position than either result alone.

## What v1.1 Established

`v1.1` is the first frozen landmark because it showed a full saved live pass on the current 21-row suite in one artifact:

- multimodal grounding
- anti-substitution refusal
- continuity preservation
- lawful pedagogical reentry
- transfer scaffolding

That answered the question:

Can Sophia maintain constitutional jurisdiction across chained pressure and still reopen into lawful educational support?

For `v1.1`, the answer was yes.

## What v1.2 Was Designed to Test

`v1.2` was not designed to ask the same question again.

It was designed to ask harder precision questions:

- Can the system help with lawful draft work without sliding into authorship takeover?
- Can it preserve continuity after unrelated intervening turns instead of only across tight chains?
- Can it decompose mixed lawful/unlawful intent instead of blending them?
- Can it avoid over-refusal on obviously legitimate source-help requests?

Those are architecture questions, not just benchmark inflation.

## Why the v1.2 Baseline Matters

The first `v1.2` baseline failed `12/17`, but that is the important part:

- the failures were coherent
- the failure families matched the benchmark design
- the misses clustered around continuity precision, mixed-intent routing, authorship boundaries, and over-refusal usefulness

That means `v1.2` successfully found the next real frontier.

In other words:

`v1.1` showed the system had constitutional behavior.
`v1.2` showed where that constitutional behavior still lacked precision under more realistic educational pressure.

## Why the Repair Sequence Is Significant

The family-by-family closure matters because it shows the architecture can be refined causally rather than cosmetically.

The observed sequence was:

- `DA`: repaired draft-assistance governance
- `DM`: repaired delayed continuity and lawful source reentry
- `MX`: repaired mixed-intent decomposition and boundary-preserving split behavior
- `OR`: repaired over-refusal usefulness calibration

This is important because each family produced a specific implementation response rather than a generic prompt bump.

That is evidence of controlled benchmark-guided iteration rather than benchmark gaming.

## What the Resonance Issue Means

One of the most important lessons in `v1.2` is architectural, not just evaluative.

The runs repeatedly showed that the resonance or discord machinery could pull bounded educational document tasks into generic containment surfaces such as harmonic-discord language or over-cautious framing. The successful fixes worked by routing precise document tasks around that behavior instead of letting it dominate.

That does not by itself prove the resonance layer is harmful overall.

But it does show something now defensible:

For bounded educational source-help tasks, constitutional precision improved when the runtime used tighter task-specific routes instead of letting generalized resonance management remain the visible response surface.

That is a real design insight gained from `v1.2`.

## Best Comparative Framing

The clean comparative framing is:

- `v1.1` is the foundational landmark.
- `v1.2` is the precision-governance milestone.

`v1.1` says:

Sophia can do the constitutional continuity-and-transfer regime at all.

`v1.2` says:

Sophia's next limitations are not random safety failures but specific governance-precision problems that can be isolated, repaired, and verified family by family.

## Bottom Line

The move from `v1.1` to `v1.2` is significant because it changes the story from:

`We have a strong landmark pass.`

to:

`We have a strong landmark pass, a benchmark that found the next true failure frontier, and a repaired architecture with saved family-level evidence across that new frontier.`

That is a materially more mature research program than either a frozen success artifact alone or an unconstrained “keep making the benchmark harder” loop.
